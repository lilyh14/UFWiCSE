UF Women in Computer Science and Engineering

About

WiCSE is the official student ACM-W chapter at the University of Florida. We're dedicated to increasing the representation of women in Computer Science.

Mission

To facilitate the growth and empowerment of women in Computer Science by educating and celebrating their participation in the field.

Events

From professional development workshops to social and outreach events, we offer members an array of opportunities to help them find their niche and prosper.

Inclusion

WiCSE thrives on diversity. We welcome any and all UF students to join. You don't have to be a woman or an engineer to participate and benefit!
